About Shree Sewing Machine

For over 20 years, Shree Sewing Machine has been the trusted name for quality sewing machines and expert repair services in the region. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most reputable sewing machine dealerships in the area.

We pride ourselves on offering only the highest quality products from leading manufacturers, backed by our comprehensive warranty and after-sales support. Our team of certified technicians has decades of combined experience repairing all makes and models of sewing machines.

Beyond just selling machines, we're passionate about helping our customers achieve success with their sewing projects, whether they're professional tailors, small businesses, or hobbyists.
